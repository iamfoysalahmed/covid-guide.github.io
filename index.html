about the app

A complete guide to fight with corona virus

This app will provide statistical data on the current state of affairs for the afflicted people in Bangladesh, including daily updates on total cases, total deaths, total recoveries, and so on.

This software is divided into seven distinct sections (Home, Covid-19, Symptoms, Precautions, Vaccine, FAQ, About Us). Each unit is holding some guidance to fight Coronavirus disease (COVID-19).

This app will inform users about the symptoms of coronavirus and will also provide information about the virus's prevention process. This app will also inform us of the status of the invented vaccine, which is currently available to the general public in Bangladesh. This application also offers answers to frequently asked questions.
